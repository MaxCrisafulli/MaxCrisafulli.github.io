Max's Top 5 Battles

Battle of Towton

The Battle of Towton was an engagement during the War of the Roses between Yorkist and Lancastrian forces. The battle was fought near the town of Towton, in Yorkshire, and was one of the final battles of the War of the Roses. The battle is described as one of the bloodiest ever fought on English soil, with the battle involving 65,000 men and resulting in 28,000 casualties.

Lee against the Union Army under George G. Meade. The Battle was the largest ever fought on American soil, and 180,000 men in total. Battle of Tannenberg

The Battle of Tannenberg was the result of a German counterattack against invading Russian forces in East Prussia. The battle involved the Eastern German Imperial Army under Paul Von Hindenburg against the Russian Army under Aleksandr Samsonov. The battle involved 380,000 men in total and resulted in 185,000 casualties.

Battle of Stalingrad

The Battle of Stalingrad was one of the largest battles ever fought, and was an attempt by the Soviet forces to stop the German forces from advancing through Stalingrad. The battle involved the German Army Group B under Friedrich Paulus against the Stalingrad Front Army under Georgy Zhukov. The battle involved 2.183 million men in total, and resulted in 1.857 million casualties, making it the bloodiest battle ever fought.
